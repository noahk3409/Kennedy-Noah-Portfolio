Swerve Drink Rebrand

Overview

A full rebrand of Swerve, a fictional milk drink line. I created new packaging, 3D bottle renders, and promotional materials to give the brand a bold, modern, and playful identity while appealing to a younger audience.

Programs Used

Cinema 4D / Redshift
Adobe Illustrator
Adobe Photoshop
Adobe After Effects
Javascript
HTML / CSS

Problem Solving

A major challenge was achieving consistent, polished lighting across every 3D render. Early results looked flat and dark and didn’t match the brand tone. I improved this by rebuilding the lighting setup, creating custom material textures, and changing render settings to fit my scene.

Earbuds Promotional Site

Overview

A promotional 3D product website built for a fictional pair of 'Ion Earbuds' designed, modelled, and animated by me.

Programs Used

Cinema 4D
Adobe Illustrator
Adobe Photoshop
JavaScript
HTML / CSS

Problem Solving

A major challenge was optimizing the 3D model viewer for web, as well as placing hotspots at the right coordinates, I fixed this by centering my model to 0 on the plane before exporting and was able to define coordinates exactly this way.
